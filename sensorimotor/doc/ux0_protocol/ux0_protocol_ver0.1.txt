
Baudrate: 1 Mbit/s

Number of motors ca. 5


5 motors: ca. 100 Byte/s = 1.000 bit/s
Freqency: 100 Hz -> 10ms, approx. com time 10% = 1m

1.000 bit/s * 1ms = 1.000.000 bit/s = 1 Mbit/s
